Industrial Applications

Carbon graphite components solving critical engineering challenges across five key industries.

WHERE WE MAKE A DIFFERENCE

The Right Material
for Every Challenge

Carbon graphite is a unique engineering material — simultaneously self-lubricating, thermally stable, chemically resistant, and electrically conductive. These properties make it irreplaceable in applications where conventional materials fail. Our components are engineered to harness these advantages across the full spectrum of industrial demand.

01
SEALING INDUSTRY

Mechanical Seals

Mechanical seals are the most demanding application for carbon graphite — requiring ultra-smooth sealing faces, dimensional precision in the micron range, and absolute chemical compatibility with the sealed fluid.

Our carbon gland rings are lapped to Ra values below 0.4 μm, ensuring leak-free operation at high pressures. They are compatible with water, oils, solvents, acids, and alkaline media.

Carbon Gland Rings Carbon Bushes Carbon Thrust Pads
✓ Zero leakage at high pressures
✓ Compatible with all process fluids
✓ No external lubrication needed
✓ Long mean time between failure
02
PUMP INDUSTRY

Pump Industry

Pumps — centrifugal, submersible, multistage, and chemical process pumps — rely on carbon graphite bushes and thrust washers as their primary wear components. The self-lubricating property of carbon is critical in pumping water, chemicals, and slurries where oil lubrication is impossible.

Our carbon bushes for pump applications are precision bored to H7 tolerances and designed to the specific shaft clearances required for each pump model.

Products Used

Carbon Bushes Carbon Thrust Pads Carbon Gland Rings
✓ Works in submerged environments
✓ Compatible with chemical media
✓ Handles high axial and radial loads
✓ Minimal maintenance requirement
03
COMPRESSOR INDUSTRY

Compressors

Rotary vane compressors and vacuum pumps are entirely dependent on the quality of their carbon vanes. The vanes must withstand continuous centrifugal forces, sliding friction against the cylinder wall, and thermal stress from compression heat — all without external lubrication.

We supply OEM-equivalent carbon vanes in standard and custom dimensions for all major compressor brands operating in pharmaceutical, food, printing, and general industrial sectors.

Products Used

Carbon Vanes Carbon Bushes Carbon Thrust Pads
✓ Oil-free compression
✓ High temperature resistance
✓ OEM replacement compatible
✓ Extended vane life
04
CHEMICAL PROCESSING

Chemical Plants

Chemical processing environments expose all components to highly corrosive acids, alkalis, solvents, and oxidizing agents. Carbon graphite's near-universal chemical resistance makes it the material of choice for pumps, agitators, and mixers handling corrosive media.

From sulphuric acid pumps to caustic soda circulation systems, our carbon components maintain dimensional integrity and sealing performance where stainless steel and ceramic alternatives fail prematurely.

Products Used

Carbon Gland Rings Carbon Bushes Carbon Graphite Blocks
✓ Resistant to acids and alkalis
✓ No contamination of process fluid
✓ Works in high-temperature reactors
✓ FDA-compatible grades available
05
INDUSTRIAL MACHINERY

Industrial Machinery

General industrial machinery — from textile machinery and printing presses to food processing equipment and material handling systems — all benefit from self-lubricating carbon graphite bearings and wear components.

In applications where contamination from lubricating oils is unacceptable, or where maintenance access is difficult, carbon graphite components offer a maintenance-free, long-life solution that reduces total cost of ownership.
