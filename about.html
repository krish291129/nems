OUR STORY

Crafting Carbon Excellence
Since 2009

CarbonTech Industries was founded in Rajkot, Gujarat with a singular vision: to manufacture carbon graphite components that meet the precision and performance demands of India's growing industrial sector.

What began as a small workshop supplying local pump manufacturers has grown into a trusted engineering partner for OEMs, chemical plants, and industrial machinery manufacturers across India and beyond.

Our deep understanding of carbon material properties — from hardness and porosity to thermal conductivity and chemical resistance — allows us to engineer solutions that outlast conventional alternatives and reduce total operating costs for our clients.

WHAT WE DO

Manufacturing Capabilities

State-of-the-art processes for precision carbon component production

01

Material Sourcing

Premium carbon graphite grades sourced from certified suppliers — electrographite, carbon-graphite, and resin-bonded grades.

02

CNC Precision Machining

Multi-axis CNC turning and milling for tight-tolerance components within ±0.01mm dimensional accuracy.

03

Surface Finishing

Lapping and grinding for ultra-smooth sealing faces required in mechanical seal applications.

04

Quality Inspection

Dimensional verification, density checks, hardness testing, and visual inspection on 100% of produced parts.

05

Custom Engineering

Custom design and reverse engineering of obsolete or non-standard carbon components from customer drawings or samples.

06

Packaging & Logistics

Secure individual packaging for fragile carbon components with full traceability for bulk orders and export shipments.
